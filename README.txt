OPTIONAL BONUS FEATURES:

- Different Audio played when player or computer Opponent collides with a candy or bead.
- Additional Items in Setting Panel:
	1) 'Opponent Initialize': Initializes a dog Opponent that has super powers (move fast and over things)
	2) 'End Competitive Game': Stops the competition, ie removes dog Opponent, and its Scoreboard
	3) 'Enter the score to end Game': The point level to which you want the player and Opponent to play till.
	    Winner is announced through an alert, then competition stops
